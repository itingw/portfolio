Hi, my name is Ting. I am an UX Engineer. I enjoy designing and building intuitive, reliable and delightful web applications with a focus on data visualization.

After studying architecture (building design), I specialized in sustainable architecture at Carnegie Mellon University because I was drawn to the data-driven approach to design. Designing a dashboard to engage users in energy savings sparked my interest in web development and data science. Since then, I have earned a nanodegree in front-end web development from Udacity and am continuing my journey as a UX Engineer at OSTHUS Services .
